CARDIO WORKOUTS

This is basially the exercises that increase your heartbeat and guys seriously you need them. Doing weights and ab exercises do not cut it here, to be honest. Believe me even before you do body weight exercises cardio workout is very much needed. Almost every anime heroes did cardio. Cardio workouts in theory is massive, but we will cover them here briefly.
There are so many cardio workouts so much so that they will all not be covered. Some make the use of machines but most are "free-lance". Basically any of these exercises such as running, jumping, crouching, burpees, etc..
Check out this video about the best way for fat loss.
In short theory, there are two types of cardio: HIIT (High-intensity interval training) and LIIT (low-intensity interval training. Basically HIIT is simply the type that you will train in a high intensive way with little or no resting. Some of them include high sprinting (either on the open field or on the treadmills in the gym), battle-ropes, cycling workouts, rowing machine, etc.
LIIT (Low-intensity interval training) this is a low impact exercise that is made up of longer resting periods.

Moreover it causes the heart to be strong for good blood circulation; it improves stamina and breathing capacity; it reduces the chance of heart attacks drastically; in addition it assist in controlling high cholestrol, high blood pressure, chances of diabetes, and most form of cancer.
