WEIGHT TRAINING

Weight taining exercises involving using equipments to bulk muscles in certain areas of the body using equipments such as barbell, dumbbell, etc. It can also be done using non-equipments which focuses your abs in increasing muscles. If you wanna be like Goku and other super anime characters, bench press is best suitable.
There are many types of weight training exercises. Some of the most famous are curls and extensions, bench press, ab exercises, etc. Let's focus on bench press.
Check out Mike Thurston's video about bench pressing.
One thing you should know is that mistakes can occur.
Some of the mistakes include widening the arm grip on the bar which damages the shoulders, locking out the elbows all of a sudden, etc.
The benefits of bench pressing is immense. The most common known benefit is the increase in strength as the muscles in the chest, upper arms, and the shoulders increase, all around the upper body. Other weight training exercises are ab workout and squats where the benefits centers on the leg region and all areas of the abs.
