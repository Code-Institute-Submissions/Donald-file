BENCH PRESS

This is a weight exercise involving both arms lifting either a barbell or a dumbbell while lying flat back on a bench.
If you wanna be like Goku and other super anime characters, bench press is best suitable.
To do this:
Lie flat either on the ground or a bench, face forward, make sure the bar is close toward the chest, and lift it up and down in number of times.
Check out Mike Thurston's video about how to do bench press properly.
One thing you should know is that mistakes can occur.
Some of the mistakes include widening the arm grip on the bar which damages the shoulders, locking out the elbows all of a sudden, etc.
Check out this video by Athlean X
The benefits of bench pressing is immense. The most common known benefit is the increase in strength as the muscles in the chest, upper arms, and the shoulders increase, all around the upper body.
Also it helps strength the lower part of the body by providing support.
It also helps in muscle improving muscle imbalance and is useful in endurance.
