SQUATS

This is one of the most common and effective exercises today.This exercise helps strengthen the legs. It involves bending down the hips from 
standing and back to normal a couple of times.
The right mechanism is that you must with the face foward and chest up and foward, bend down like you are
sitting down on a chair, and move back to normal standing. Squats can be done using a bar or other weights.
Check out this video illustrating how squats are performed by Athlean X.

But a squat must be properly done to not only build strength in the muscles but to also prevent strain and fatigue. Improper squats or over-worked squats can cause injury
Check out this video detailing how to do it.

Benefits of squats are that it builds muscles using a list of muscles in the legs;
using the leg tendons will increase strength; provides good mobility;
It also help in improving health by burning fat.
