PUSH UPS

This core exercise involving your face, hands, and legs. It builds up your chest, arms and most upper muscles of the body. This is one of the favs of some of anime characters.
To do this, just put your palms on the floor, face looking down, and legs straight.

But you need to do it properly so you build strength and muscles and prevent strain and fatigue. Remember to warm up before doing it.

Check out this video detailing how to do it.

This helps in build up of the triceps and biceps, the upper arm, and the chest. It can also help in loosening fat and increases strength. There are variants of push ups.
