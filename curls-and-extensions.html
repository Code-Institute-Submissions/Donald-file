CURLS and EXTENSIONS

The bicep curls is a famous weight training exercise that works effectively on the biceps of the upper-arm as well as the lower arm.
Tricep extensions is even more effective as it involves the entire tricep muscles to maximise arm strength.
Doing the bicep curls is easy as it involves using either the dumbbells, barbells, or the cable curls. All these can be done using weight ranges.
Check out this video by Athlean X describing the concept of bicep workout.
Tricep extension is a whole lot different as the pull up involves the movement on the triceps. The is more challenging than bicep exercises arguably.
