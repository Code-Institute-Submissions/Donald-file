VEGETA

Character Intro
Goku's main rival. Once the crowned prince of Planet Vegeta, Vegeta became an anti-hero after defecting from Frieza's team. He has developed an hunger to become stronger than Goku at every turn increasing his rivalry with Goku. With this he has trained to become stronger than he was before. Just like Goku, he has joined forces with his friends to protect Planet Earth and the universe from some of the most powerful evil enemies starting with the most dreaded tyrant, Frieza.
